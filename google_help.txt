TROUBLESHOOTING: Finding Google OAuth Settings

1. Open this URL: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/credentials
   (Make sure you are logged in to the correct Google Account)

2. Check the "Project" dropdown in the top-left corner (next to the Google Cloud logo).
   - Ensure "Breakpoint Billiards" (or your specific project name) is selected.

3. Look for the list "OAuth 2.0 Client IDs".
   - You should see "Android Client" or similar.
   - Click the Pencil Icon (Edit) on the right side of that row.

4. Scroll down to "SHA-1 Certificate fingerprint".
   - Click "ADD FINGERPRINT".
   - Paste the new SHA1 into the box.
   - Click SAVE.
